# Student weekly academic planning

Trigger: the weekly planning appointment. Output: a realistic weekly schedule with daily starting actions and a recovery process.

The complete redesigned sequence is generated from `studentIntake` and contains: commitment and deadline collection; assignment identification; assignment decomposition; effort estimation; dependency identification; prioritization; realistic scheduling; daily starting actions; unfinished-work review; and following-week update.

Required assets include weekly intake, assignment decomposition, effort estimation, priority framework, weekly plan, daily kickoff, missed-task recovery, and end-of-week review.

Boundaries: official sources remain authoritative; the student owns priorities and capacity decisions; private health or disability information stays out of unapproved AI tools.
