Create a clear 90-minute lecture for first-year students

## Student-level adaptation

- Prerequisites are stated
- Jargon is defined
- Examples match course level
- Scaffolding precedes independent work
- No identifiable student data is used

## Academic review

- Claims trace to approved sources
- Citations and calculations are verified
- Fact and interpretation are distinguished
- Objectives, activity, and assessment align
- Final files and permissions are checked

## Risk notes

- Review AI-assisted output for accuracy before use.
- Do not include student records or identifiable student work in external AI tools.
